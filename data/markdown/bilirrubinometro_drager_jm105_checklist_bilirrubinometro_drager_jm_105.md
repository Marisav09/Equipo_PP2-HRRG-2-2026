GENERAL

Locate the Jaundice Meter JM-105 within the unit.

Perform the Light Checker procedure.

State whether the Checker range is acceptable for use.

Perform the proper cleaning procedure prior to use.

Locate the ON/OFF switch and turn the unit ON.

Note the unit of measure: mg/dL or  µ mol/L.

Enter the patient’s information.

Demonstrate how to take a reading.

State where on the infant’s body the reading will be taken.

Flag a patient with the priority flag.

Look up a patient file in the  HISTORY.

Turn the unit OFF.

Explain what the device displays when the measurement is out of range.

Replace the Jaundice Meter JM-105 on its charging cradle and explain what the orange light means.
